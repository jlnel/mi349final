Drunk

Jazz fusion mixed with soul and some incredible bass work. Such a playful album but also emotionally complex and adventurous.

Smooth neo soul with genre blending production. A great listen that explores of vulnerability, love, and personal growth.

Very unique guitar work and experimental production. This album has a fresh take on modern indie soul that pushes boundaries and is utside the box.

Indie Pop / Bedroom Pop

Classic indie pop with dreamy melodies. It's timeless and is just as cool as it can get to be honest.
