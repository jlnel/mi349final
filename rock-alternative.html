Rock & Alternative

Indie tunes and crushing heavy anthems thats spans across the spectrum of alternative music.

Alternative / Indie Rock

Mitski

Puberty 2

Raw indie rock with devastating emotional honesty. The powerful songwriting feels like a deep cut as the ablum progresses.

Alex G

Rules

Experimental indie with lo-fi charm. There's an unpredictable yet suprisingly relatable vibe because of how deeply personal the songwriting is.

Radiohead

The Bends

Alternative rock at its finest with intense guitar and Thom Yorke's (the lead singer) mesmerizing vocals to create the timeless anthems of this album.

Mac DeMarco

This Old Dog

Mellow indie with vintage feeling. This album has such a unique vibe and genuinely feels comforting.

Current Joys

Wild Heart

Dreamy type of rock with nostalgic vibes. This entire album has that coming of age feeling that's relatable and realistic.

Post Hardcore / Nu Metal / Emo

Korn

Follow The Leader

Nu metal masterpiece that's honestly defined a generation. It's heavy, aggressive, and groundbreaking.

Loathe

I Let It In and It Took Everything

Modern heavy music at its peak. Somehow this album has so many different elements of nu metal, hardcore and shoegaze and made them all mesh perfectly.

Sleeping With Sirens

Let's Cheers to This

Post hardcore that feels so personal and meaningfull by exploring relatable things like personal struggles, dreams, and relationships.

Paramore

Riot!

Pop punk perfection with explosive energy. There's so many iconic hooks in this album plus your ears are blessed by Hayley Williams' powerhouse vocals.

Mindless Self Indulgence

You'll Rebel to Anything

Chaotic electronic punk that breaks all the rules. This album is all over the place and that's what makes it so fun.
